✦ Agreement Notice

By joining, playing, or participating in RedLine, you automatically agree to follow all rules listed on this page. Ignorance is not an excuse. Staff decisions are final.

  • Treat all players and staff with respect.
  • No NSFW content, hate speech, or harassment.
  • No spamming, advertising, or self-promotion.

⟡ SMP Gameplay Rules
  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs.
  • No griefing, stealing, or scamming.
  • PvP must be fair and mutual.
  • Respect community builds and claims.
